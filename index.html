View Research Get in Touch scroll 01 ABOUT I'm Muhammad, a BS Physics student at the International Islamic University Islamabad (IIUI), working under the supervision of Dr. Sardar Sikandar Hayat — a specialist in DFT and materials science. My research focuses on first-principles electronic structure calculations, using Density Functional Theory (DFT) to understand how electrons behave inside crystalline materials at the quantum level — without any empirical parameters. I run simulations on Quantum ESPRESSO v7.2 on Linux (WSL2/Ubuntu), visualize charge densities in VESTA, and build computational physics tools like LatticeLab — a Python toolkit featuring Ising models, molecular dynamics, and lattice simulations.
